About Us

Two Decades of
Real Estate Excellence

Founded in 2004 by siblings Alexander and Victoria Crane, Prestige Estates began as a boutique Manhattan brokerage with a simple philosophy — treat every client like family and every property like it's your own.

Today, we're a team of 50+ dedicated agents, analysts, and concierge specialists serving clients across the United States and Canada. Our intimate size means you never get lost in the shuffle; our reach means we can find you the perfect property anywhere in North America.

We don't just sell homes — we curate lifestyles. Every listing in our portfolio undergoes rigorous vetting for location, architectural integrity, and investment potential before it ever reaches our site.
